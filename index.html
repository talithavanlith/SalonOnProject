Welcome to SalonOn

This is the home of a COMP523 team software engineering project called SalonOn. The aim is to create an app named 'SalonOn' that will match stylists with empty salon chairs and customers with stylists.

The purpose of this site is to provide all the information needed to understand our project, our plans, and our progress.
